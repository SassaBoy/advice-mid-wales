BECOMING A VOLUNTEER

To help us better understand your suitability and ensure that volunteering with us will be a mutually beneficial experience, we have a few steps we like to follow. Please rest assured that all of these stages are meant to be informal and relaxed.

1

Application Form

This is your chance to give us a comprehensive picture of your background, interests, and motivations for volunteering. The more detail you provide, the better we’ll be able to determine whether we should proceed with an informal chat. This helps ensure we’re only moving forward if we feel there’s a good potential fit for both you and us.

2

Initial Chat

If your application is shortlisted, we will invite you to an initial virtual chat. This will usually be a short video call, and having your camera on is important to help us verify your identity. It’s a great opportunity for us to get to know each other and for you to learn more about us.

3

Informal Interview

If the informal chat goes well and it feels like a good fit, you may then be invited to a follow-up interview involving a senior member of our team and/or the Project Manager. This step allows us to discuss the role in greater detail and ensure the position aligns with both our needs and your expectations. If successful, we’ll provide you with more information on the next steps, including formal registration, DBS check, induction, and training.

BENEFITS OF VOLUNTEERING WITH US

IMPACTFUL EXPERIENCE

Make a real difference by supporting those in need, building empathy and contributing to your community.

CV BUILDING

Gain valuable, hands-on experience that enhances employability, particularly in fields like social work, counselling and legal services.

CAREER EXPLORATION

Gain clarity about your future career path through practical, trial-and-learn experiences in our supportive environment.
